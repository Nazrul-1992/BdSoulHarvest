About Our Goal

Our community journey started in Germany through cricket. In Frankfurt, every year we organize cricket tournament called Frankfurt Premier League or FPL which include around 150 to 200 Bangladeshis all together and after a certain period this community gathering actually changed the whole Bangladeshi community structure. Community gathering always creates a huge impact in creating bonding, unity and brotherhood. People who are coming new to Germany pursuing their studies and jobs are also found so benefitted through this FPL journeys and It’s a long-term effect in the community. Talking about cricket it’s always about young generation but if we want to integrate other people, families and Bangladeshi kids born in Germany nothing can be a better platform than a cultural event, may be concerts. Cultural events and fests are a great stand to combine people from different generations and ages so that everyone can come, know each other and at the end of the day the whole community gets benefitted for the long run. Bangladeshi kids who born in Germany never get the opportunities to experience Bangla cultural fest or concerts. So BD Soul Harvest is trying to display our Bangla cultural events to them so that they can get familiar with Bangla culture and activities. BD Soul Harvest encourages people who have musical potentialities to sing or playing instruments so that they can have a platform to disclose their musical talents. And in this process our Frankfurt Bangladeshi band named “Band of Brothers” born and currently there are 6members in that band. So many entrepreneurs are currently having business in online or offline in a small scale. BD Soul Harvest emphasizes to promote them in the cultural events by displaying their items in the cultural fests. So with some noble aspects, we BD Soul Harvest started our journey towards the common welfare of community around Frankfurt, Hessen and Germany..

SAALBAU Stadthalle Bergen

Frankfurts östlichster Stadtteil setzt sich zusammen aus Bergen und Enkheim. Hier liegt im Stadtrandgebiet mit sehenswertem historischem Stadtkern und direkt neben dem barocken Wasserschlösschen „Die Schelmenburg“ die SAALBAU Stadthalle Bergen. Sie erreichen das Haus citynah mit öffentlichen Verkehrsmitteln sowie über die A 66 oder die B 3. Parkplätze sind vorhanden.
